SimpleTask

Welcome to SimpleTask, the simple to-do list and task manager!

SimpleTask has one mission: to keep things simple, so you can focus on the important things.


To create a new task, just add the task's name, deadline, estimated length to complete, and (optionally) select its priority.

With this information, SimpleTask will automatically create your personalized, daily to-do list designed to evenly spread your workload, optimize your performance, and let you focus on what needs to be done.

Add a task, see your to-do list, or see a full list of features!
